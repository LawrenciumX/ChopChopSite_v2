Who We Are

The Hands Behind ChopChop

Jean Claude Ndoumbi

Co-Founder & Executive Producer

A former line cook turned storyteller, Jean Claude spent a decade in restaurant kitchens before turning the camera on the people who taught him. He sets the tone for every ChopChop story — patient, curious, and never in a rush to get to the plate.

Ruth Ndoumbi

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Ruth built her eye for light and texture shooting documentary food stories from Ghana to Canada. She leads ChopChop's visual language — the slow pans, the steam, the close-ups on hands — turning every kitchen into a place worth lingering in.

"The finest meal is not measured by its price, but by the hands that shaped it and the soul it nourishes."

— Jean Claude Ndoumbi
